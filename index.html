Here we give an oveview of the most intriguing and intersting topics in detail. Science is all about finding its mysteries. It includes from the biggest galaxies in depth of space to the smallest sub-atomic particles in an atom. Math is the heart of Physics. It is a language to describe the beauty of physics. "When you change the way you look at things, the things you look at change !", said someone. You really need to think in terms of physics to understand the symmetry and charm of physics. ou are always living in the past. Everything you are experiencing right now actually happened 80 milliseconds ago. Random errors are inevitable you cannot avoid them when you are able to avoid them completely then it means your brain is working perfect at instants don't worry. Everyone seems to have fixed ideas about the nature of time. Time isn't a single line, moving from past to future. Let's say I go back in time and kill someone we'll call "A". This alters something called the divergence number, and the world line moves to a world without "A". When there's a world line move, people's memories are altered. In other words, memories of the world line where A existed are deleted and replaced with memories of a world line where A doesn't exist.
